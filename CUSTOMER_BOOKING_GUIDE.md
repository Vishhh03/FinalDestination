## Understanding Room Availability

### How Rooms Are Calculated

**Guests to Rooms Conversion:**
- 1-2 guests = 1 room
- 3-4 guests = 2 rooms
- 5-6 guests = 3 rooms
- And so on...

**Example:**
- You book for 5 guests
- System needs 3 rooms
- Hotel has 10 total rooms
- 5 rooms already booked for your dates
- Result: 5 available rooms (enough for your booking)

### Overlapping Bookings

The system checks for date overlaps:
- Your check-in is before another booking's check-out
- Your check-out is after another booking's check-in
- These rooms are considered "occupied"

**Example:**
- Hotel has 10 rooms
- Booking A: Jan 1-5 (2 rooms)
- Booking B: Jan 3-7 (3 rooms)
- You want: Jan 4-6
- Available: 10 - 2 - 3 = 5 rooms

## Troubleshooting

### "No Rooms Available"
**Problem:** Availability check shows no rooms
**Solutions:**
- Try different dates
- Reduce number of guests
- Check nearby hotels
- Book further in advance

### "Check-in Date Invalid"
**Problem:** Cannot select check-in date
**Solutions:**
- Ensure date is today or future
- Check date format
- Refresh the page

### "Booking Failed"
**Problem:** Error during booking confirmation
**Solutions:**
- Verify all fields are filled
- Check availability again
- Ensure you're logged in
- Try again in a few minutes

### "Payment Required"
**Problem:** Booking confirmed but not complete
**Solutions:**
- Go to "My Bookings"
- Find the booking
- Click "Pay Now"
- Complete payment process
